## About Paul Brothwood

My name is Paul Brothwood. I am a Chartered Environmentalist (CEnv), Operations Manager and adventure motorcyclist from the United Kingdom.

In June 2026 I was selected to join Honda Adventure Roads 2026. Riding the Honda XL750 Transalp through the Pyrenees alongside riders from around the world was one of the most rewarding experiences of my motorcycling journey.

This page brings together my photographs, articles and experiences from Honda Adventure Roads 2026.
